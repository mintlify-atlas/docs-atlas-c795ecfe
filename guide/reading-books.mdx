Kafka provides dedicated readers for PDFs and EPUBs, allowing you to read books and documents with a tailored experience for each format.

## Opening Books for Reading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Book">
    Browse or search for books. Look for items marked as PDF or EPUB in their media type indicator.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Item Details">
    Tap on the book to view its details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Reading">
    Tap the primary action button (labeled "Read" or "Open") to begin reading. The appropriate reader will open based on the file type.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Alternatively, you can:

- Access the files list by tapping the download icon
- Filter to show only PDF or EPUB files
- Tap a specific file to open it in the reader

<Tip>
  Download books before reading for a faster, more reliable reading experience, especially for large PDFs.
</Tip>

## PDF Reader

The PDF reader is optimized for reading PDF documents and scanned books.

### PDF Reader Features

- **Page Navigation**: Swipe vertically to move between pages
- **Zoom Controls**: Pinch to zoom in and out
- **Page Counter**: View current page and total pages
- **Auto-Resume**: Automatically returns to your last read page
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Continuous page scrolling for comfortable reading

### Reading PDFs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open PDF">
    Tap a PDF file to open it in the PDF reader.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate Pages">
    Swipe up and down to move through pages. The current page number appears as you scroll.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Zoom for Detail">
    Pinch to zoom in on text or images. Pan around the zoomed page by dragging.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Controls">
    Tap the center of the screen to show or hide the reader controls and page information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### PDF Reading Tips

- **Page Position Saving**: Your current page is automatically saved every few seconds
- **Large PDFs**: For better performance with large PDFs, download the file first
- **Scanned Pages**: Use zoom to read scanned text clearly
- **Landscape Mode**: Rotate your device for wider pages or two-page spreads

<Note>
  PDF page numbers are saved automatically. When you return to a PDF, it opens to your last position.
</Note>

## EPUB Reader

The EPUB reader provides a customizable reading experience for EPUB ebooks.

### EPUB Reader Features

- **Reflowable Text**: Text adjusts to your screen size and preferences
- **Font Customization**: Change font family, size, and line height
- **Background Colors**: Choose from multiple background color options
- **Chapter Navigation**: Navigate through book chapters seamlessly
- **Reading Settings**: Customize your reading experience

### Reading EPUBs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open EPUB">
    Tap an EPUB file to open it in the EPUB reader.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read and Scroll">
    Scroll vertically to read through the book. Text flows naturally based on your screen size.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Tap anywhere on the screen to open the reading settings sheet.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Reading Experience">
    Adjust font size, font family, or background color from the settings sheet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Customizing EPUB Settings

The EPUB reader offers extensive customization options:

#### Font Size

- Drag the font size slider to adjust text size
- Choose from 7 different font sizes (12sp to 24sp)
- Line height adjusts automatically with font size
- Preview changes in real-time as you adjust

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the screen to show the settings sheet.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Font Size">
    Use the slider under "Font Size" to make text larger or smaller.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview Changes">
    Changes apply immediately - continue reading to test the new size.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Font Style

Choose from four font families:

- **Serif**: Traditional, classic reading font (default)
- **Sans Serif**: Modern, clean font for easy reading
- **Monospace**: Fixed-width font, useful for technical content
- **Cursive**: Decorative font for a unique reading experience

Tap any font name to switch to that font family.

#### Background Color

Select from multiple background colors optimized for reading:

**Light Mode Options:**
- White (default surface)
- Light gray
- Warm cream/sepia
- Cool gray
- Ivory white
- Soft pink

**Dark Mode Options:**
- Dark surface (default)
- Medium gray
- True black
- Deep charcoal
- Warm dark
- OLED black

<Tip>
  For reduced eye strain, try the warm cream background in light mode or the OLED black background in dark mode.
</Tip>

### EPUB Reading Tips

- Settings are modal - adjust them, then tap outside to continue reading
- Text automatically reflows when you change settings
- Font and background combinations can improve reading comfort
- Larger fonts are great for reading in bright light or at distance
- Serif fonts are generally easier to read for long-form content

<Note>
  EPUB settings apply to the current reading session. Your preferences are remembered for future sessions.
</Note>

## General Reading Features

### Progress Saving

Both readers automatically save your progress:

- **PDFs**: Current page number is saved
- **EPUBs**: Reading position within chapters is tracked
- Progress updates every few seconds while reading
- Resume from the homepage "Continue Reading" section

### Continuing Your Reading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Book">
    Books you've started appear in "Continue Reading" on the homepage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quick Resume">
    Tap the book card to immediately resume reading from your last position.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Or From Item Details">
    Navigate to the book's details page and tap "Continue" to resume.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Progress is indicated by a visual progress bar on the "Continue Reading" cards.

### Reading Modes

**Online Reading** (Streaming):
- Reads directly from the Internet Archive
- Requires internet connection
- Instant access
- No storage used

**Offline Reading** (Downloaded):
- Download files to your device
- No internet needed
- Faster page loads
- Reliable access
- Uses device storage

For offline reading instructions, see the [Offline Usage guide](/guide/offline-usage).

## Reader Controls

### Common Controls

- **Tap Center**: Show/hide controls and settings
- **Swipe**: Navigate through content
- **Pinch** (PDF only): Zoom in/out
- **Back Button**: Exit reader and save progress

### System Integration

- Use your device's back button to exit the reader
- Status bar can be hidden for immersive reading
- System brightness controls affect reader display
- Rotation lock can keep orientation fixed

<Tip>
  Enable your device's blue light filter or night mode for comfortable evening reading.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Reading Issues

### File Won't Open

- Check your internet connection for online reading
- Try downloading the file first for offline reading
- Ensure the file format is PDF or EPUB
- Some very large files may take time to load

### PDF Pages Not Loading

- Download the PDF for better performance
- Check available storage space
- Try a different quality version from the files list
- Close and reopen the reader

### EPUB Formatting Issues

- Try adjusting font size and family
- Some EPUBs may have embedded formatting that overrides settings
- Restart the reader if text doesn't reflow properly

### Progress Not Saving

- Ensure you're signed in (even anonymously)
- Check that storage permissions are granted
- Don't force-close the app while reading
- Allow the app to run in the background

<Note>
  For the most reliable reading experience, download books for offline use and ensure adequate device storage.
</Note>
