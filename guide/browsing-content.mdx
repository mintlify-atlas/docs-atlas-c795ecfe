Kafka provides multiple ways to discover and browse content, from curated collections on the homepage to powerful search capabilities.

## Homepage

The homepage is your starting point for discovering content. It features:

- **Featured Items**: Carousel of highlighted audiobooks and texts
- **Continue Reading**: Quick access to items you've recently opened
- **Curated Collections**: Organized rows and grids of content by subject, creator, or theme
- **Authors & Creators**: Browse content by notable creators
- **Subject Tags**: Explore content organized by topics and genres

### Navigating the Homepage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Homepage">
    Tap the **Home** icon in the bottom navigation bar to view the main feed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Featured Content">
    Swipe through the featured carousel at the top to see highlighted items. Tap any item to view its details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Continue Reading">
    If you have recently accessed content, you'll see a "Continue Reading" section near the top with your progress indicated by a visual shelf.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Collections">
    Scroll down to browse various collections organized by subjects, recommendations, or themes. Each collection can be displayed as rows, grids, or columns.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse by Creator">
    Tap on any author or creator card to see all their available works.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter by Subject">
    Tap subject tags (displayed as genre chips) to view all content related to that topic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Long-press on any item in the "Continue Reading" section to remove it from your recent list.
</Tip>

## Search

The Search page provides powerful tools to find exactly what you're looking for.

### Searching for Content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Tap the **Search** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Search Query">
    Type your search term in the search bar at the top. You can search by title, creator, subject, or keywords.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filters">
    Use the filter chips below the search bar to refine your results:
    - Select search filters (e.g., exact matches, broad searches)
    - Choose media types (audiobooks, PDFs, EPUBs, texts)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Results appear in a grid layout. Tap any item to view its details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Recent Searches

When you first open the Search page, you'll see your recent searches. Tap any previous search to quickly repeat it, or swipe to remove searches from your history.

<Note>
  The search includes voice input support on supported devices - tap the microphone icon in the search bar to search by voice.
</Note>

### Search Tips

- **Broad searches**: Use general terms to discover more content
- **Specific searches**: Include creator names or exact titles for precise results
- **Filter combinations**: Combine search filters with media types for targeted results
- **Subject searches**: Search by topic or genre to explore thematic collections

## Content Discovery Features

### Recommendations

Kafka provides personalized recommendations based on your browsing and reading history. These appear in dedicated sections on the homepage.

### Subject Collections

Content is organized into subject collections, making it easy to explore specific topics:

- Browse by genre (fiction, non-fiction, science, history, etc.)
- Explore by format (audiobooks, texts, periodicals)
- Discover by language or region

### Creator Pages

Tap on any creator name to view:
- All available works by that creator
- Creator information and biography
- Related creators and works

<Tip>
  Use the "Share App" prompts throughout the interface to share Kafka with others and help grow the community.
</Tip>

## Navigation Tips

- **Bottom Navigation**: Quickly switch between Home, Search, and Library
- **Profile Access**: Tap your profile icon in the top-right corner of the homepage
- **Back Navigation**: Use your device's back button or the in-app back button to return to previous screens
- **Direct Access**: Tap on any item card to view its details and access reading/listening options

<Note>
  The homepage content updates regularly with new featured items and recommendations. Pull down to refresh the feed.
</Note>
