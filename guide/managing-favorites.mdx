---
title: Managing Favorites
description: How to save and organize favorite items in Kafka
---

Kafka's favorites feature lets you save items you love and want to access quickly. Your favorites sync across devices when you're signed in.

## Adding Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find an Item">
    Browse or search for any book, audiobook, or text you want to save.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Item Details">
    Tap the item to view its details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the Heart Icon">
    Tap the heart icon in the action row at the bottom of the screen. The heart fills in to indicate the item is favorited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The item is instantly added to your favorites and will appear in your Library.

<Tip>
  You can favorite items before downloading or reading them - use favorites as a "want to read" or "want to listen" list.
</Tip>

## Viewing Your Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Library">
    Tap the **Library** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Favorites Tab">
    The Library has two tabs: "Favorites" and "Downloads". The Favorites tab is the default view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Your Collection">
    Scroll through your favorited items. They appear in the order you added them (most recent first).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Favorites Layout

You can switch between two viewing modes for your favorites:

### List View

- Shows items with full details (cover, title, creator, media type)
- Larger item cards for easy browsing
- Displays description previews
- Best for discovering details about favorited items

### Grid View

- Compact grid of item covers
- See more items at once
- Faster scrolling through large collections
- Best for quickly finding a specific item by cover

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Layout Toggle">
    At the top of the Favorites list, you'll see the layout toggle.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Switch Views">
    Tap the layout icon to toggle between list and grid views.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your layout preference is remembered for future visits.

## Removing Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Item Details">
    Tap any favorited item to view its details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the Filled Heart Icon">
    Tap the filled heart icon in the action row. The heart becomes outlined to indicate the item is no longer favorited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The item is immediately removed from your favorites list.

Alternatively, you can unfavorite from the Library:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Library Favorites">
    Navigate to the Library > Favorites tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the Item">
    Tap on the item you want to remove to open its details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Unfavorite">
    Tap the filled heart icon to remove it from favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Removing an item from favorites doesn't delete any downloaded files. Downloaded content remains in the Downloads tab.
</Note>

## Favorites and Syncing

### Without Sign-In

You can use favorites without signing in:

- Favorites are stored locally on your device
- They persist across app restarts
- Won't sync to other devices
- Will be lost if you uninstall the app

### With Sign-In

When you sign in to Kafka:

- Favorites automatically sync to the cloud
- Access your favorites from any device
- Favorites are preserved even if you uninstall the app
- Sign in on a new device to see all your favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    From the Library > Favorites tab, if not signed in, you'll see a prompt to "Log in to sync favorites".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Sign-In Method">
    Tap the prompt and sign in with Google or create an account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Sync">
    Your local favorites automatically sync to your account after sign-in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Sign in with Google to enable favorites syncing across all your devices without creating a separate password.
</Tip>

## Organizing Your Favorites

### Using Favorites as Lists

You can use favorites for different purposes:

- **To Read/Listen Queue**: Favorite items you plan to consume soon
- **Research Collection**: Save reference materials and research sources
- **Recommendations**: Keep track of recommendations from friends
- **Archive**: Save completed items you want to revisit

### Managing Large Favorite Collections

- Use search to find specific favorited items
- Switch to grid view for faster visual browsing
- Periodically review and unfavorite items you no longer need
- Remember that downloads are separate - unfavoriting doesn't delete files

## Favorites in Item Details

On any item's details page, the favorite status is shown by:

- **Outlined heart**: Item is not favorited
- **Filled heart with primary color**: Item is favorited
- **Animation**: Heart animates when toggled

The heart icon is always visible in the action row, making it easy to favorite or unfavorite while browsing.

## Finding Favorited Items

### From Library

The primary way to access favorites:

1. Tap Library in the bottom navigation
2. View all favorites in list or grid layout
3. Tap any item to open its details

### From Search

You can also find favorited items through search:

1. Search for the item by name or creator
2. Favorited items show a filled heart in search results
3. Tap to open the item

### Quick Access

Items you're actively reading/listening to appear in "Continue Reading" on the homepage, regardless of favorite status.

<Note>
  Favorites and "Continue Reading" are separate features. An item can be favorited without being started, or started without being favorited.
</Note>

## Favorites Best Practices

- **Favorite liberally**: There's no limit to the number of favorites
- **Use as a discovery tool**: Favorite items while browsing to review later
- **Sync early**: Sign in before accumulating many favorites to avoid data loss
- **Combine with downloads**: Favorite and download items for offline access
- **Regular curation**: Periodically review your favorites and remove completed items

## Empty Favorites State

If you haven't added any favorites yet:

- The Library > Favorites tab shows "No favorites items" message
- Browse the homepage or search to find items to favorite
- Tap the heart icon on any item to add your first favorite

<Tip>
  Start by favoriting a few items you're interested in - this helps you build a personal library within Kafka.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Favorites

### Favorites Not Syncing

- Ensure you're signed in with the same account on all devices
- Check your internet connection
- Sign out and sign back in to force a sync
- Contact support if issues persist

### Lost Favorites After Reinstall

- Favorites are only preserved if you were signed in
- Sign in with the same account to restore synced favorites
- Local-only favorites (without sign-in) cannot be recovered

### Can't Add to Favorites

- Ensure the item details page has loaded completely
- Check that you have internet connectivity
- Try signing in anonymously if you haven't yet
- Restart the app if the heart icon is unresponsive

<Note>
  Kafka automatically creates an anonymous account when you first use features like favorites, allowing progress and favorites to be saved even without explicit sign-in.
</Note>
