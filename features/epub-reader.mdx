Kafka's EPUB reader provides a comfortable reading experience with extensive customization options. Adjust fonts, text size, background colors, and more to match your reading preferences.

## Opening EPUB Files

Start reading EPUB books from the app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an EPUB item">
    Find an item that contains EPUB files through search or browse. EPUB files are common for fiction and non-fiction books.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your file">
    On the item detail page, tap an EPUB file to open it in the reader. The file will load and display the book content.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for parsing">
    EPUB files are parsed when first opened. A progress indicator shows while the book is being prepared for reading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  EPUB files must be downloaded before reading. The download happens automatically when you open an EPUB for the first time.
</Note>

## Reading Interface

The EPUB reader displays text in a clean, book-like format:

- **Chapter view** - Content flows continuously through chapters
- **Justified text** - Text is justified for a traditional book appearance
- **Custom fonts** - Choose from multiple font families
- **Adjustable size** - Scale text to your preferred reading size
- **Theme options** - Select background colors for different lighting conditions

### Navigation

- **Scroll to read** - Scroll vertically through the book content
- **Continuous flow** - Chapters flow seamlessly from one to the next
- **Tap for settings** - Tap anywhere to access reader settings

<Tip>
  The EPUB reader remembers your scroll position, so you'll always return to where you left off.
</Tip>

## Customization Settings

Personalize your reading experience with comprehensive customization options. Tap anywhere on the page to open the settings sheet.

### Font Size

Adjust text size for comfortable reading:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Tap anywhere on the reading screen to show the settings sheet at the bottom.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find font size control">
    Look for the "Font Size" section with a slider control and preview text ("Aa" in small and large sizes).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust size">
    Drag the slider left to decrease or right to increase text size. Changes apply immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Available Sizes

- **12sp** - Extra small (line height: 18sp)
- **14sp** - Small (line height: 18sp)
- **16sp** - Medium (line height: 24sp) - Default
- **18sp** - Medium-large (line height: 26sp)
- **20sp** - Large (line height: 28sp)
- **22sp** - Extra large (line height: 30sp)
- **24sp** - Maximum (line height: 32sp)

<Note>
  Line height adjusts automatically with font size to maintain comfortable readability.
</Note>

### Font Style

Select the font family that's most comfortable for your eyes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Serif" icon="font">
    Traditional book-style font with decorative strokes. Best for long-form reading. (Default)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Sans Serif" icon="font">
    Clean, modern font without decorative strokes. Good for clarity and quick reading.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Monospace" icon="code">
    Fixed-width font where all characters are the same width. Useful for technical content.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Cursive" icon="pen-fancy">
    Flowing, handwriting-style font. Adds a personal touch to reading.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open font settings">
    Tap the screen to show settings, then locate the "Font Style" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview fonts">
    Each font is shown with its name in the actual font style. Tap any option to preview it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your preference">
    Tap your preferred font. The change applies immediately to all text.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Background Color

Choose a background color that's comfortable for your current lighting conditions:

#### Dark Mode Backgrounds

When using dark mode, select from:

- **Surface** - Default dark surface color (follows system theme)
- **Surface Variant** - Alternative dark surface
- **Dark Gray** (#2C2C2E) - Softer dark gray
- **Charcoal** (#1C1C1E) - Deep charcoal
- **True Black** (#121212) - Pure black for OLED screens
- **Warm Dark** (#3B3B2E) - Dark with warm tone
- **Deep Black** (#0D0D0D) - Deepest black

#### Light Mode Backgrounds

When using light mode, select from:

- **Surface** - Default light surface color (follows system theme)
- **Surface Variant** - Alternative light surface
- **Light Gray** (#F5F5F5) - Soft gray
- **Sepia** (#FAF3E0) - Warm, cream color (reduces eye strain)
- **Cool Gray** (#E9E9E9) - Neutral gray
- **Ivory** (#FFFFF0) - Off-white with warm tone
- **Blush** (#FDF1F1) - Very light pink tint

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access background settings">
    Tap the screen and scroll to the "Background" section in settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View color options">
    You'll see a horizontal row of color swatches. The currently selected color has a highlighted border.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Try a color">
    Tap any color swatch to apply it. The background changes immediately so you can see how it looks with your content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Sepia (light)** and **Dark Gray (dark)** are popular choices that reduce eye strain during extended reading sessions.
</Tip>

## Saving Your Preferences

Your reading preferences are automatically saved:

- Settings persist when you close and reopen books
- Preferences apply to all EPUB files you read
- Each customization setting is independent
- Changes take effect immediately

<Card title="Quick Settings Access" icon="sliders">
  Tap anywhere on the reading screen at any time to access settings. Tap outside the settings sheet to close it and return to reading.
</Card>

## Reading Progress

Kafka automatically tracks your reading progress:

- **Scroll position** - Your exact position in the text is saved
- **Chapter tracking** - Resume reading in the correct chapter
- **Automatic bookmarking** - No need to manually save your place
- **Cross-device sync** - Reading position syncs when signed in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reading automatically saves">
    As you scroll through the book, your position is continuously saved in the background.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close anytime">
    Exit the reader at any point using the back button or navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resume later">
    When you reopen the book, you'll return to your exact scroll position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accessibility Features

The EPUB reader supports various accessibility needs:

- **Large text** - Scale up to 24sp for improved visibility
- **High contrast** - True black and white options for maximum contrast
- **Custom fonts** - Choose fonts that work best for dyslexia or other reading needs
- **Text reflow** - Content automatically adjusts to your font and size settings

<Note>
  Text-to-speech and other system accessibility features work with the EPUB reader content.
</Note>
